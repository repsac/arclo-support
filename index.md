# Arclo Support

Arclo is a one-tap swing arcade game. You control two orbs joined by a short tether and tap in rhythm to thread a line of gates while a void chases you from behind.

This is the support and documentation site. Pick what you need:

- **[User Guide](user-guide.md)** - a complete walkthrough of every mode, tier, power-up, and setting.
- **[Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md)** - quick fixes for sound, purchases, leaderboards, iCloud sync, and more.
- **[Changelog](changelog.md)** - what changed in each release.
- **[Privacy Policy](privacy.md)** - what Arclo does and does not do with your data.

## Contact

Questions, bugs, or feature requests? Email [nospam@edcaspersen.com](mailto:nospam@edcaspersen.com). To help us act on a report quickly, please include your device model, your iOS version, and the app version (shown under Settings > About inside Arclo).
